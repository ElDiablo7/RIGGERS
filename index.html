The story

Rigging on a live production isn’t just “putting metal in the air” — it’s managing risk, time, access, noise, change requests and tight schedules. Redbeard works with production, lighting, camera and art departments to keep installs safe, efficient and predictable.

  • Safety‑first, alwaysPlanned loads, safe access, correct hardware, and documented sign‑off.
  • Set‑ready workflowWe know the pace — calm comms, quick pivots, tidy work areas.
  • Production‑friendlyWe work with departments, not against them — no ego, no chaos.

How it works

Simple process: enquiry → brief → quote → booking → install → handover.
